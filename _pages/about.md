## Welcome to the KOALA Lab 🐨

The **KOALA** (**K**nowledge of **O**ptimization and **L**earning **A**lgorithms) Lab is at
the [School of Computational Science and Engineering](https://cse.gatech.edu/) at
**Georgia Institute of Technology**, led by [Professor Kai Wang](https://guaguakai.com).

We develop **artificial intelligence, machine learning, optimization, and multi-agent systems**
to create social impact — specifically in health, public health, and environmental sustainability.

---

## Research Themes

- **Decision-Focused Learning & Differentiable Optimization** — End-to-end learning that
  reasons about downstream decisions, integrating ML with combinatorial optimization.
- **Restless Multi-Armed Bandits** — Scalable algorithms for resource allocation under
  uncertainty, with deployments in maternal/child health and wildlife conservation.
- **Algorithmic Game Theory** — Stackelberg games, security games, and Nash equilibrium
  computation for strategic multi-agent settings.
- **AI for Social Impact** — Deployed systems for maternal health (ARMMAN), pediatric early
  warning (Children's Healthcare of Atlanta), and environmental monitoring.
