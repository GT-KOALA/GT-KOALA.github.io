We develop theory and algorithms for **decision-focused learning** — training ML models whose
outputs feed directly into optimization problems, trained end-to-end with respect to decision
quality rather than prediction error.

**Key directions:**
- Fully first-order methods for bilevel and linearly-constrained optimization
- Differentiable optimization layers for neural networks
- Predict-then-optimize for sequential decision problems (MDPs, RL)
- Compact quality-aware surrogate learning
